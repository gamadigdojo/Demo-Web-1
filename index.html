About Us

Starbucks Corporation is a multinational coffee company and coffeehouse chain based in Seattle, Washington. It was founded in 1971 and has since grown to become one of the largest coffee chains in the world, with over 30,000 stores in 80 countries.

Committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing, and has a long-standing reputation for providing excellent customer service and a welcoming environment for its customers.
